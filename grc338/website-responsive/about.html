About

Our Story

Skylar cafe was created in 2010 when, at the tender age of 2, our founder Skylar first showed her prodigious chef skills. We then sprung into action as friends and family across the US asked for a restaurant at which they could sample Skylar's creative food inventions. So here we are, a decade later, with 20 Michelin stars for our little cafe.
